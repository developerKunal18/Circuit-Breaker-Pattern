🧠 Why This Is Important

Without Circuit Breaker:
Service A
    ↓
Service B (Down)
    ↓
Timeout
    ↓
Application Slow

With Circuit Breaker:
Service A
    ↓
Circuit Breaker
    ↓
❌ Service B Down

Immediate Response

Benefits:
✅ Prevents cascading failures
✅ Faster error handling
✅ Better system stability
✅ Improved user experience

Netflix Hystrix

Spring Cloud
